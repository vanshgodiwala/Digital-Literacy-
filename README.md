Summary:

The project focuses on digital literacy and responsible use of
technology through multiple practical tasks. It begins with creating an
infographic using Canva, highlighting the importance of digital
literacy, useful tools, and safe internet practices. It also explains
platforms like LinkedIn, GitHub, and Kaggle, emphasizing their role in
building skills, networking, and career growth.

The project further explores hands-on learning through the HackerRank
"Hello World" task and Google Forms, showing how these tools help in
coding practice and efficient data collection. It also includes a
scenario demonstrating the importance of professional digital
communication.

Finally, the project reflects on cybersecurity awareness, especially UPI
fraud, and emphasizes the need for caution and responsible online
behavior. Overall, it highlights how digital skills, communication, and
safety are essential for students in today's digital world.
